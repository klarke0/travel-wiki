## Top Recommendation So Far

**Manzanita** edges out Cannon Beach for a relaxed adult trip in August — significantly less crowded, cheaper rentals ($197–$350/night with hot tub vs. $600–$700 in CB), and the same beach quality. Cannon Beach is 20 min north if you want to see Haystack Rock.

**Best rental find:** Neahkahnie Beach House ([vrbo.com/1047915](https://www.vrbo.com/1047915)) — Finnish outdoor sauna + 5-person hot tub + ocean views, 163/166 "Excellent" reviews.
