## Booking Tips

- **Call agencies directly** for 2-night Sunday–Tuesday availability — platform minimums (often 6 nights in July–August) don't always apply to mid-week slots, and agencies have flexibility they won't advertise online
- **Ask specifically about Sunday Aug 23 check-in** — some properties require Saturday check-in in peak season, which would add an unwanted night
- **Nehalem Bay State Park yurts:** Reservations open 6 months prior — book Feb 23, 2026 at 6:00 AM at [reserveamerica.com](https://www.reserveamerica.com). August availability disappears within minutes of opening
- **Hot tub priority?** Call Manzanita Beach Getaway (503-368-2929) first — they maintain a hot tub category and know which properties have 2-night Sunday availability. Coast Cabins Spa Cabin #5 is the only hotel hot tub option
- **Add taxes and fees to all estimates:**
  - Manzanita lodging tax: 9%
  - Airbnb/VRBO platform fees: 10–14%
  - Local agency fees: 3–7% (Beachcombers NW: 0%)
  - Vacasa fees: ~12%
- **August pricing reality:** Published nightly rates are often low-season floors. Expect August rates to run 30–60% higher than the advertised minimums. The estimates in this guide use August assumptions where data was available
- **Minimum stay workarounds:** Mid-week Sunday–Tuesday sometimes qualifies for 2-night exceptions even when platforms show 6-night minimums. Always call before ruling out a property
- **Coast Cabins Spa Cabin #5** is likely already booked for August — call 503-368-7113 as soon as possible if the hot tub hotel option is the target
